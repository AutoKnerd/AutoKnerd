# Design System: AutoKnerd Executive Architecture
**Project ID:** 5782919484502870319

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design system is **"Precision Luxury."** It strikes a balance between the company's bold, mechanical heritage (the gear and glasses) and an executive-level architectural aesthetic. The mood is high-performance and authoritative, utilizing deep blacks, ultra-fine mechanical lines, and a signature "Precision Green" accent that ties directly to the brand identity. It feels like a high-end engineering firm—clean, calculated, and elite.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Obsidian Black (#020202):** The primary background color, providing a deep, cinematic canvas.
*   **Precision Green (#7CFF1B):** The brand's signature accent color. Used for high-impact CTAs, system status indicators, and subtle data pulses to signify "Logic & Action."
*   **Titanium White (#F8FAFC):** The primary text color, crisp and legible.
*   **Soft Platinum (#94A3B8):** Used for secondary text, technical labels, and structural lines.
*   **Frosted Slate (rgba(148, 163, 184, 0.03)):** Used for card surfaces with high backdrop-blur to create a material quality that feels high-end.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Primary Font (Headers):** Elegant, high-contrast sans-serif (**Outfit**). Headlines use generous tracking (letter-spacing) for an airy, luxurious feel.
*   **Technical Font:** Sophisticated monospace (**JetBrains Mono**) for functional readouts and monospaced labels.
*   **Hierarchy:** Dramatic contrast between large, thin headlines and small, spaced-out technical labels.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Navigation:** A floating glass bar with the official AutoKnerd logo. Links use uppercase tracking and subtle green underlines on hover.
*   **Executive CTAs:** High-saturation Precision Green backgrounds with black text. Hovering creates a soft, expansive green glow.
*   **System Cards:** Large modular containers with "Frosted Slate" properties. Corners are rounded (3.5rem) for a contemporary look.
*   **Technical Visuals:** Ultra-fine "blueprint" grids and pulsating green data points that feel like high-end radar or telemetry.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Spacious Intent:** Massive vertical whitespace between sections to signal high-value information.
*   **Asymmetrical Balance:** Modern, non-centered layouts that feel intentionally designed.
*   **Micro-interactions:** Slow, intentional fades and subtle scaling that reinforce the "Engineered" methodology.
